ABOUT ME

Allow me to introduce myself.

Hi, my name is Talbot Grimes, but everyone just calls me The Blight. I used to be just like any other chemist, except I've always been fascinated with how even the smallest of plants could have strong health effects, both positive and negative. After contributing to sciences I have studied the art of running very fast, but only in a straight line directly into a wall or your face. These days I like to relax with a nightly stroll into the woods where I sacrifice survivors to The Entity in a death game. Some people have told me that I should find a more normal hobby, like knitting, but I just can't see myself doing anything else.

I've been sacrificing survivors to the entity since 2020. Always in a rush, I'm a busy guy.
